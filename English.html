About us

Greedy Education is an organization established by UBC students, focusing on providing training and support to high school students in Vancouver. Greedy Education also serves as a community dedicated to sharing insights and experiences related to studying abroad. Joining us means connecting with alumni from various majors at UBC. As seasoned upperclassmen and upperclasswomen, we are here to offer assistance and guidance to help you navigate any challenges or uncertainties you may encounter during your study abroad journey.

Business Introduction

 High School

Greedy Education offers courses and tutoring in all high school subjects, including AP (Advanced Placement). We understand that the academic pressure college admissions in Vancouver is increasing every year. Our teachers not only provide academic support to help students excel in their studies but also offer free consultations and assistance to students who are in the college application process.

*To ensure educational outcomes, we will provide personalized instruction for each student. All teaching will be presented in a one-on-one format.

 Pre-Univeristy

Greedy Education provides college-level courses and tutoring for first-year college students. By gaining a head start in college-level knowledge, students can delve deeper into their areas of interest and gain clarity about their academic direction in college.

*To ensure educational outcomes, we will provide personalized instruction for each student. All teaching will be presented in a one-on-one format.

 More

Greedy Education can not only assist you academically but also provide support throughout your study abroad journey!
